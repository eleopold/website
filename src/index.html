Info

Are you a cyborg? This guy looks like he is but in reality he just a curious and motivated hacker person. Just like the wireless hacker person next to him.

If you're curious and motivated too, a hackathon is the place for you. Join hundreds of students from across the midwest to try new things, challenge your creativity, and "HACK" something together.

We define HACKING as using technology in a way that it was not originally intended.

Form a team ahead of time or join one when you get here. Learn how to program, construct robots, and build electronics and then use your knowledge to build a project. Workshops will be held during the event to teach you everything you might need to know. Work directly with our corporate sponsors to build relations in addition to your amazing project.

Yeah, does this sound cool?

Join us for the COOLEST hackathon around! We encourage students from schools across the midwest to register. It's totally free!
